Artist: Van Gogh

Work of art: Sunflowers

Year: 1888

This is one of the most well-known art work of all time. I once tried imitate this painting, and in that process I realized why this is such a great work. The overall color is yellow and orange, but if we zoom in the photo, we can see there are a lot of extra colors, such as blue on the right side. Van Gogh also uses various shades of green, brown, and yellow, which adds vitality to the painting. The hardest part to imitate is not just the delicate color combinations, but also the strokes he used. We can see he used very small but powerful strokes to depict the blooming of the sunflowers.
這是一幅舉世聞名的畫作，我曾經試圖模仿過這幅畫，而在過程中才真正體會到這個作品的不容易。作品的整體顏色是黃色和橘色為基調，但也參雜了藍色等其他顏色。同色系顏色的選擇上非常多元，這讓整個作品富含生命力。另外一個難以模仿之處在於筆觸，每一道筆觸短而有力，充分展現太陽花的盛放。
