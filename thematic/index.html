The topic for my thematic website

The topic for this website will is to introduce classical art from different eras. The content will include Renaissance Art, Romanticism Art, and Modern Art. I will first give an introduction on the unique features of the artworks from those eras, then I will choose a few iconic works to discuss more in depth. The discussion includes the basic information regarding the artworks, an close analysis, and my own intepretation towards them.
