Artist:John William Waterhouse

Work of art: The Soul of the Rose

Year: 1908

This painting, at first glance, is a woman admiring the roses in her garden. However, ater taking a closer look, I feel like the woman is showing a sense of pain. The feature was drawn to the smallest details, and we can see that she may be longing or wanting on something. Roses are sweet, but she is waiting upon something sweeter.
這幅畫初看到時，乍看只是一位婦女在庭院內賞花，但看得仔細一點的話，會發現她似乎陷在某種苦痛當中。她的面部表情被很仔細的描繪，而這使我們看到她的表情似乎透漏出一種渴望和等待的心情。玫瑰花縱然很美，但那位婦女可能再等更勝於花的人事物。
