Artist: Raffaello Sanzio da Urbino ; Raphael

Work of art: The school of Athens

Year: 1509–1511

In this painting, it depicts a group of philosopher, wise men, and artists are either arguing with each other, or contemplating in silence under the Basilica. To me, this painting resembles the situation in classes nowadays. For example, the guy half laying on the stairs while reading, resembles the classmates who didn't study much but still get good grades. The one on the bottom left corner, looks like those who are always copying other people's homework right before the deadline. Also, the one on the right upper corner, who is standing by a pillar while staring at the person besides him. He resembles the kind of classmate, with no apparent reason, loves to keep a constant watch on what others are doing. This painting makes me connect to a modern day class, creating a connection between past and present, which is what makes me so interested in this work.

這幅畫作所描繪的是一群哲學家、藝術家以及有智慧的人們，聚集在教堂內激烈的討論和靜靜的思考。對我而言，這幅畫的有趣之處在於裡面的人物和現代生活中的學生們有著很高的相似性。例如，畫面中間一位臥坐在階梯上的人，那慵懶的姿勢很像班上那些不太認真卻輕易拿到高分的同學們。畫面左下角， 有一位很雷同在作業期限前最後一天，趕緊抄作業的同學，而右上角探討探腦的人物，則很像一些總愛盯著別人做事的同學。
