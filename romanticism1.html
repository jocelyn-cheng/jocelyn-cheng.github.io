Artist: Caspar David Friedrich

Work of art: Wanderer above the Sea of Fog

Year: 1818

Every time I look at this painting, I feel as if I were bought into a world of dreams. The clouds just the man's foot was not drawn in a very realistic way, thus making this scene more vague and dreamy. The way the man is standing tall and looking sown on all the mist and clouds, is like encourging us to hold our heads high in the face of the unkown future.
每當我看到這幅畫時，都敢被引入到一個夢境中。靠近那個人腳下的雲是採以一種虛虛實實的畫法，而這讓整個情境更虛幻。畫中的人筆直的展在高處眺望那些雲，讓我感覺仿佛是在激勵我們，在面對充滿無知的未來時也要勇敢應對。
