Artist: Johannes Vermeer

Work of art: The Milkmaid

Year: 1657–1658

Vermeer is known for capturing lights and showing lights through his work. In this painting, the bright and dark color do not have a strong contrast between them, but we can still clearly see the light is coming from the window. Through subtle shading, this work paints a calming yet vital scene.
維梅爾是著名的光影大師，他時常透過自己的作品來傳達光影變化。在這個作品中，亮色和暗色區域並沒有很強烈的區別，但我們仍可清楚看見光線是從窗戶投射進來，而這也讓畫作既平靜又充斥了生命力。
